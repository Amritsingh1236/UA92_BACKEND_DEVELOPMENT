About Us

We are a private, co-educational school located in the heart of the city. Our mission is to provide a nurturing and challenging environment where students can develop their full potential.

We offer a range of programs from kindergarten to grade 12, including an International Baccalaureate program and a focus on STEM education.

We have a highly qualified and dedicated faculty, many of whom hold advanced degrees in their fields.
